# CanSat Team Member Agreement

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
This document outlines the mandatory requirements, behavioral expectations, and financial obligations for participation in the CanSat Project. By agreeing to these terms, the Team Member acknowledges their commitment to the project's goals, timelines, and collaborative standards.

## 2. DURATION AND EXPIRATION
This agreement is valid from the date of signing and shall remain in full force until the occurrence of any of the following events:
1. **Competition Conclusion:** The official end of the CanSat competition.
2. **Elimination:** The formal elimination of the team from the competition.
3. **Project Termination:** The mutual decision by the team or leadership to dissolve the project.
Upon expiration, all active behavioral and time commitments are terminated, though financial contributions remain non-refundable.

## 3. FINANCIAL AND RESOURCE OBLIGATIONS
As a condition of membership, each Team Member is strictly required to provide and maintain the following:
1. **Financial Contribution:** A mandatory, non-refundable contribution of $30 USD to fund project expenses.
2. **Equipment:** A personal laptop in working condition, capable of handling project-related tasks.
3. **Connectivity:** A reliable and stable WiFi connection to ensure uninterrupted communication and work.
4. **Mobility:** The ability and willingness to commute to the designated project laboratory as required.

## 4. TIME AND AVAILABILITY COMMITMENT
To ensure the timely progress of the project, Team Members must adhere to the following time commitments:
1. **Weekly Hours:** A minimum dedication of six (6) hours per week strictly focused on project tasks.
2. **Weekly Meetings:** Mandatory attendance at weekly status meetings to report on individual progress and overall project state.
3. **Foreseen Absences:** In the event of an unavoidable conflict (e.g., academic exams, personal emergencies) that severely impacts a Team Member's ability to contribute, especially near critical project milestones, the Team Member is strictly required to notify the team at least one (1) week in advance.

## 5. ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
1. **Task Assignment:** Each team member will be assigned specific technical and/or administrative roles. Members are expected to complete their deliverables on time and to a professional standard.
2. **Accountability:** If a member is struggling to meet a deadline, they must inform the team immediately rather than waiting until the deliverable is due.

## 6. BEHAVIORAL AND COMMUNICATION STANDARDS
Open, honest, and proactive communication is essential.
1. **Communication Channels:** Official project communication will occur through the designated team channels. Members are expected to acknowledge messages within 24 hours.
2. **Decision Making:** The team will strive for consensus on major design and project decisions. If a consensus cannot be reached, a majority vote will be taken.
3. **Withdrawal or Loss of Interest:** If a Team Member experiences a loss of interest or anticipates an inability to continue with the project, they are obligated to communicate this immediately to the team leadership, rather than delaying the notification.

## 7. CONFLICT RESOLUTION AND ENFORCEMENT
1. **Dispute Resolution:** In the event of a conflict or if a member fails to meet expectations, the team will first attempt informal mediation. If unresolved, it will escalate to a formal team review.
2. **Termination:** Failure to comply with any of the stipulations outlined in this agreement, or persistent failure to deliver on assigned responsibilities, may result in immediate review of the Team Member's status and potential expulsion from the project.

## 8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND WITHDRAWAL
1. **Ownership of Work:** All work, including but not limited to code, designs, documentation, and hardware configurations produced by any Team Member for the CanSat Project, is the sole intellectual property of the team.
2. **Post-Withdrawal Rights:** In the event that a Team Member withdraws, is expelled, or otherwise ceases participation in the project, all intellectual property created during their tenure remains the property of the team. The departing member agrees to provide all necessary files, passwords, and documentation to ensure project continuity.

## 9. AMENDMENTS
1. **Contract Modifications:** The team reserves the right to add, modify, or remove terms and conditions in this agreement as the project evolves. Any amendments require a majority agreement from the team and will be communicated to all members.
